Financials(XFJ)

Companies involved in activities such as banking, mortgage finance, consumer finance, specialised finance, investment banking and brokerage. asset management and custody, corporate lending, insurance, and financial investment, and real estate, including REITs.

Materials(XMJ)

Companies with a range of commodity-related manufacturers, included in this sector are companies that manufacture chemicals, construction materials, glass, paper, forest products and related packaging products, and metals, minerals and mining companies, including producers of steel.

Health Care(XHJ)

Contains companies that manufacture equipment & supplies, provide health care related services, or owners/operators of products, facilities and organisations. This includes companies involved in research, development, production and marketing of pharmaceutical & biotech products.

Real Estate(XRE)

Comprises stocks included in the S&P/ASX 200 that are classified as members of the GICS real estate sector.

Industrials(XNJ)

Businesses in Manufacturing & Distribution of capital goods, incl. aerospace&defense, construction, engineering & building, equipment & machinery; or Commercial Services & Supplies incl. employment, environment & office; or Transportation Services incl. airlines, marine and road & rail.

Consumer Staples(XSJ)

Manufacturers and distributors of food, beverages and tobacco and producers of non-durable household goods and personal products. It also includes food & drug retailing companies as well as hypermarkets and consumer super centers.

Consumer Discretionary(XDJ)

Businesses in automotive, household durable goods, textiles and apparel and leisure equipment. The services segment includes hotels, restaurants and other leisure facilities, media production and services, and consumer retailing and services.

Energy(XEJ)

Businesses in the construction or provision of oil rigs, drilling equipment and energy related service & equipment, incl. seismic data collection; or,companies engaged in exploration, production, marketing, refining and/or transportation of oil & gas products, coal and other consumable fuels.

Communication Services(XTJ)

Provides investors with a sector benchmark that reflects those companies included in the S&P/ASX 200 that are classified as members of the GICS communication services sector and sub-industries.

Utilities(XUJ)

Companies considered to be in electric, gas or water utilities, or companies that operate as independent producers and/or distributors of power.
